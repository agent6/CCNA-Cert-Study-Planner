# Academy CCNA Study Planner

A smart, interactive study planner that transforms the Cisco CCNA certification roadmap into daily, manageable tasks tailored to your schedule.

## Features

- **Customizable Schedule**: Choose your study days and set your own timeline (16, 26, or 36 weeks, or custom)
- **Task Management**: Track progress with an intuitive checkbox interface
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress indicators showing completed tasks and remaining work
- **Drag & Drop**: Reschedule tasks by dragging them between days
- **PDF Export**: Generate downloadable study plans
- **Month Overview**: Calendar view of your entire study schedule
- **Local Storage**: Your plan is saved automatically in your browser

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser
2. Select your preferred study days
3. Choose a start date
4. Pick a timeline (16, 26, 36 weeks, or custom)
5. Click "Save plan" to generate your personalized study schedule

## Features in Detail

### Plan Settings
- **Study Days**: Select which days of the week you want to study
- **Start Date**: Choose when you want to begin your CCNA preparation
- **Timeline Options**: Pre-set timelines or custom deadline
- **Auto-calculation**: Automatically distributes tasks across your available study days

### Progress Management
- **Edit Plan**: Modify your study schedule and dates
- **Generate PDF**: Export your plan for offline reference
- **Reset Progress**: Clear all completed tasks (only visible when you have progress)

### Task Views
- **Yesterday/Today/Tomorrow**: Quick view of recent and upcoming tasks
- **Month Overview**: Calendar visualization of your entire study plan
- **Task Details**: See topic areas and estimated study time for each task

## Technical Details

Built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. No external dependencies required.

- **Storage**: Uses browser localStorage for data persistence
- **Responsive**: Optimized for desktop and mobile devices
- **Accessible**: ARIA labels and semantic HTML for screen readers
